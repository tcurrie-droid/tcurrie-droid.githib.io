About

TANEIL
CURRIE.

I'm Taneil Currie, a business consultant, growth marketer, and entrepreneur with over two decades of experience helping businesses work better. I've built and sold two companies, led marketing at a SaaS startup, and consulted for founders across multiple industries.

What I kept seeing, over and over, was the same problem: brilliant people stuck in broken systems. Too much time on the wrong things. Growth stalling not because the idea was bad, but because the operations couldn't keep up.

That's exactly what I help fix. Whether it's tightening up your strategy, overhauling messy workflows, or building AI automations that take the grunt work off your plate, I bring a grounded, practical approach that gets results without overcomplicating things.

The right strategy creates momentum. The right systems create capacity. Together, they create a business that actually works.
